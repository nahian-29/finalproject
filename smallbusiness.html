A customer is most attracted to businesses that not only satisfies their needs but also provides a high quality service which propels them to go back and consume more. In order for a start-up business to catapult themselves towards success, the business needs to consider the following:

  • Get organized, plan, and set goals
  • Make an e-commerce site with an official page of the business
  • Leverage social media
  • Create local awareness and establish a network
  • Offer coupons or free products and services on special occasions.


A small business owner's primary tool to grow business would be to take every opportunity to market his business. This can be done by attending networking events, sponsoring a local event or charity, maanaging public relations, using the power of social media presence, hosting seminars and events, and becoming a strong public speaker.
