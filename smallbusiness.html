What is Investment? Investment refers to the use of money or capital to buy certain assets which has a great probability to incur a profitable return rate of our money. It can be either a short-term or long-term investment. However, investing involves volatility and patience for a lifetime or as long as one chooses to keep investing. There are several ways one could start investing:

  1. Try the cookie-jar approach
  2. Invest as much as possible in the stock market
  3. Get involved with real-estate market
  4. Invest in low-initial-investment mutual funds
  5. Play it safe with Treasury Securities


Why should one invest? Millions of people work '9-5' jobs, take extra shifts, do not take off during holidays, and so on in order to increase their financial worth. Some work hard to get that promotion for the extra pay while some chase money influx so hard that they forget all about their personal lives and health. Investing is a method where one can build their finances using the money they earn. In other words, using money to make more money.
